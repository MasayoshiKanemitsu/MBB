Marketa's Bed & Breakfast

Located in the heart of downtown Victoria, Marketa's Bed and Breakfast offers a unique taste of traditional Edwardian comfort and hospitality, mixed with European flair. Inside the heritage home built in 1912, guests can experience the romance of hand-crafted four poster beds and air jetted tubs to beautifully decorated family-style rooms.

Choose from private or shared baths and queen or four-poster beds. Our B&B accommodations offer the personal service and ambience of a bed and breakfast that our guests expect with an inn's charm.

So come, reserve a room at Marketa's Bed and Breakfast and see one of Victoria's best kept secrets for yourself.

At Marketa's Bed and Breakfast you will find Edwardian comfort at every turn. The warmth of wood, from paneled walls to four poster beds (ornately decorated by Marketa herself) infuses this bed and breakfast treat with its calming influence. Each room is individually decorated and has special features and personal touches (including Marketa's own artwork).Whether you are visiting us on a family break, a romantic getaway, or business, our uniquely decorated rooms will enchant you. We have rooms to fit most budgets and can accommodate various size groups of all age's even our younger guests are catered to with baby cribs and rollaway beds.For that special weekend Marketa's Bed and Breakfast invites you to try one of our feature rooms. Experience the luxury of a beautiful four-poster bed, fireplace and jetted tub in our Edwardian heritage house.

Marketa's Edwardian heritage house bed and breakfast is very popular, and as it is within a few minutes of Victoria's lively inner harbour, great shopping and many great attractions. It gets booked up quickly throughout the year, so, to secure your booking we ask that reservations are held with a Visa or MasterCard.

The rates posted on this page are for double occupancy. Federal and provincial taxes are not included. Please call us about rates for single occupancy, students, seniors, small group events and weddings. We will do everything we can to accommodate you!
